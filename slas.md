# Understanding {{site.data.keyword.cloud_notm}} service level agreements and availability targets
{: #slas}

Learn about {{site.data.keyword.cloud}} service level agreements (SLAs), availability targets, and service credits for cloud services that fail to meet stated availability, ensuring high uptime guarantee.
